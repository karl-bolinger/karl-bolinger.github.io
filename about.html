Profile

Hi, I'm Karl with a K, aka Star-Lord. I live and work by three principles: see a need, fill a need, leave it better than I found it, and 'First clean the inside of the cup and dish, and then the outside also will be clean.' I am an independent problem-solver with over 10 years experience building solutions for businesses to operate more efficiently and effectively. I've worked with and at many businesses, from fledgling startups to established, large organisations. I'm also an artist and  a budding coder.

Availability

I am open and available for projects. Reach out, as I’m always interested to hear of new opportunities, so please get in touch, it would be great to hear from you.

Background

I have a Masters in IT from NCAT with a focus in Data Management & Engineering. (AGGIE PRIDE!). Originally, I studied Fine Art at Clemson University with a focus on Scultpture & Photography. I've picked up additional knowledge ranging from Computer Science and Product Developement from Georgia Tech and  UF.

Shortly after, I obtained certifications from Udcacity on Front-End Development & Data Engineering. My transition into the Tech Community is continous. My goal is to blend my creativity and tech-saviness to build solutions for the individual and for the business unit.

How I work

I either work embedded within a team as a freelance technical program manager, helping establish and scale a digital product or solution, or as a small one man agency designing and building solutions for clients.

I'm always interested to hear of new projects and collaborations, so feel free to get in touch.
